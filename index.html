About

I am driven by questions about structure. I am particularly interested in understanding how low-dimensional geometric or functional structures emerge from high-dimensional physical and data-driven systems.

My recent explorations include manifold-based representations, operator perspectives such as Fourier analysis, and topological viewpoints on complex datasets. I am motivated by the idea that rigorous mathematical frameworks can reveal hidden simplicity within seemingly complex systems.
